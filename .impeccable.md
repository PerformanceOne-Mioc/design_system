# Design Context — Dennis Mioc, Performance Marketing

## Brand Voice
**Three words:** Präzise · Datengetrieben · Verlässlich  
(Precise, data-driven, reliable)

The brand projects quiet authority. It does not shout. KPIs speak louder than headlines. Every visual choice reinforces the impression that the person behind this work has already thought three steps ahead.

## Target Audience
Performance marketing clients — primarily mid-to-large e-commerce brands and direct-to-consumer companies. Decision-makers who are intelligent but time-constrained; they evaluate work at a glance and want numbers to be unambiguous. The interface is both a working tool and a trust signal.

## Design Decisions (Rationale)

### Fonts
**Barlow Semi Condensed** (display) + **Atkinson Hyperlegible** (body).

Barlow SC: condensed industrial grotesque. Creates horizontal economy in data-heavy layouts, projects authority at large KPI sizes. Not in the impeccable banned-font list.  
Atkinson: designed for maximum legibility, each letterform is distinctively shaped to prevent misreading. Perfect for data tables, labels, and descriptions where misreading a number is costly. Not in the banned-font list.

Both fonts were chosen after rejecting: Syne, IBM Plex Sans, DM Sans, Space Grotesk, Instrument Sans (all banned reflex fonts).

### Color Logic
Warm amber/copper (`oklch(68% .155 60)`) as the single accent. Chosen because:
- Amber connotes precision, warmth, and confidence without corporate coldness
- It reads as distinctly owned — not interchangeable with any large agency's blue
- All neutrals are amber-tinted at very low chroma (0.007–0.013) for subconscious cohesion

Light + dark mode both implemented via `data-theme` on `<html>`, localStorage-persisted, with `@media (prefers-color-scheme: dark)` as a no-JS fallback.

### Fluid Glass Variant
A second visual register for client presentations, impact slides, and executive dashboards. Derived from reference images of organic amber/indigo glass sculptures. Cool light-gray ground, organic animated blobs in the background, frosted glass panels with backdrop-filter. Accent shifts to warm orange (`oklch(59% .23 33)`) — the saturated focal point of the organic color story.

The color gradient story for all fluid glass elements: **purple → dusk mauve → peach → sand → orange** (cool shadow → warm transition → saturated focal point).

### Aesthetic Direction
"Editorial precision meets performance dashboard." Not the medical/corporate cold-gray school of data visualization; not the loud colorful startup school either. Closest reference point: the interior of a precision instrument — warm metal, matte surface, clear readings, no decorative noise.
